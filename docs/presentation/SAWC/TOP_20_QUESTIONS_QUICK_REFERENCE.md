1. **"Which dressing type shows the best healing rates for diabetic foot ulcers?"**

2. **"Are compression bandages more effective than compression stockings for venous leg ulcers?"**

3. **"Do pressure injuries respond better to negative pressure therapy or standard wound care?"**

4. **"What's the success rate of different debridement methods for sloughy wounds?"**

5. **"What's the average healing time for diabetic foot ulcers treated in our facility?"**

7. **"Which patient factors predict poor wound healing outcomes?"**

8. **"Are our surgical site infection rates improving over time?"**

---

### Resource Utilization & Cost

9. **"What's our average cost per healed wound by wound type?"**

10. **"How many dressing changes does the average pressure injury require before healing?"**

11. **"Which wounds consume the most nursing time per week?"**

### Quality & Risk Management

12. **"What percentage of our pressure injuries are hospital-acquired vs. present on admission?"**

13. **"Are we documenting wound assessments consistently across all shifts?"**

14. **"What's our readmission rate for patients discharged with wounds?"**

15. **"Which patients have wounds that aren't healing as expected?"**

---

### Population Health & Trends

16. **"What's the prevalence of diabetic foot ulcers in our patient population?"**

17. **"How has our wound care quality improved since implementing the new protocol?"**

18. **"What's the infection rate for wounds by anatomical location?"**

---

### Operational Efficiency

19. **"How many patients are currently being treated for pressure injuries across all facilities?"**

20. **"What percentage of wounds have clinical photos documented at each visit?"**
