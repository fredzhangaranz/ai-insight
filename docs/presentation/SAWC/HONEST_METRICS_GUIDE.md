## âœ… Final Checklist: Before Using Any Metric

Before including any claim in poster, presentation, or conversation, verify:

- [ ] Can I cite a credible source? (Industry benchmark, published research)
- [ ] Is this based on our actual product capabilities? (Not aspirational features)
